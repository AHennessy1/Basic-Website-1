Hi there, Have you been feeling stressed, anxious, or low? Perhaps you’re struggling with difficult changes or decisions, feeling stuck, or noticing that something doesn’t quite feel right. You might simply be looking for someone outside of your situation — a space where you can talk openly and be met without judgement.

You don’t need to have clear goals or know exactly what you want from counselling. Some people come wanting practical support and direction; others want time to reflect, process, and make sense of their experiences. Many want a combination of both.

If you’re looking for a warm, collaborative approach that adapts to you — rather than expecting you to fit a particular model — my way of working may be a good fit.
