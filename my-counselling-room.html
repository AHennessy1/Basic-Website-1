My Counselling Room

A Safe Space for Therapy

This room is where I previously worked with clients in person. While I’m not currently offering face-to-face sessions, I’m sharing these images to give a sense of the kind of space I value in my work — calm, private, and thoughtfully held.

Whether we meet online or in person in the future, creating a space that feels safe, grounded, and welcoming is central to how I work. My aim is to offer the same sense of care and containment in online sessions, allowing you to settle, reflect, and engage at a pace that feels right for you.
